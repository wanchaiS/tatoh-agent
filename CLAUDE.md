## Design Context (Cooper / Tatoh)

### Users
Two audiences: **Guests** (chat interface, phone/tablet, relaxed vacation mode) and **Staff** (knowledge management, desktop, during shifts).

### Brand Personality
Quietly luxurious. Three-word anchor: **composed · precise · warm**

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark. Deep warm charcoal + cream text + warm gold accent.
- **Palette (OKLCH)**:
  - Background: `oklch(0.18 0.01 60)` — deep warm charcoal
  - Text: `oklch(0.92 0.015 80)` — cream white
  - Accent: `oklch(0.75 0.12 75)` — warm gold
  - Border: `oklch(0.28 0.01 60)` — subtle charcoal lift
  - Muted: `oklch(0.55 0.01 70)` — mid-gray warm
- **Previous cream palette is fully REPLACED** — do not build on it
- **Reference**: Linear/Notion density + luxury hospitality warmth
- **Anti-reference**: Current cream-background look; generic SaaS dashboards

### Mascot
Pixel art cat on main chat page (`/`). Idle animation at rest; full animation (mouth, tail, ears) while AI is streaming. ~32×32 or 48×48 grid, warm gold/cream palette.

### Design Principles
1. Premium through restraint — dark surfaces + warm gold, no glows/gradients/neon
2. The mascot is the personality — UI around it stays clean
3. Typography carries the brand — no Inter, no Geist, no Syne
4. Trustworthy first — solid and reliable, not playful or casual
